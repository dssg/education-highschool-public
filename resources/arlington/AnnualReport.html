APS Annual Report

The 2013-14 APS Annual Report is available for the community and highlights the achievements of our students. It showcases the great strides that the school division and community have made together in moving APS closer to realizing the goals in the 2011-17 Strategic Plan. Each department highlights programs, provides data, and explains how decisions are linked to the Strategic Plan. The report provides explanations about why the data is important, and how it will continue to guide our school division toward continuous improvement as we move forward.
